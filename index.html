Setting up in Microsoft Planner
1
Open Microsoft Planner at teams.microsoft.com → Plans → New plan
2
Create one plan per trade show in a Teams Group called Trade Shows 2026, using these exact names:
FAIR – IPW – Fort Lauderdale – 2026
FAIR – ITB China – Shanghai – 2026
FAIR – Arabian Travel Market – Dubai – 2026
FAIR – FIT Latin America – Buenos Aires – 2026
FAIR – Arival 360 – Spokane – 2026
FAIR – TTG Travel Experience – Rimini – 2026
FAIR – ITB Asia – Singapore – 2026
FAIR – Cancun Travel Mart – Cancún – 2026
FAIR – Antalya Tourism Fair – Antalya – 2026
FAIR – WTM London – London – 2026
3
In each plan, create 3 buckets: PRE-SHOW, DURING, POST-SHOW
4
Add the tasks for each bucket with the assigned person (see Tasks by show tab) and set the due date based on show dates
5
Enable notifications: Settings → Notifications → Task assignments & due dates
6
Pin the plan to a Teams channel: + Add a tab → Planner → Use existing plan
Use colour labels: green = done, yellow = in progress, red = blocked
Recommended deadlines
PRE-SHOW
Deadline: 2 weeks before the show
DURING
Deadline: each day of the show (recurring daily tasks)
POST-SHOW
Deadline: Monday of the week after the show closes
